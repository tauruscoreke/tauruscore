A self-built tech company that started by fixing phones and grew into a full technology solutions brand serving Kenya and beyond.

Tech that works for real people.

TaurusCore was born from a simple belief: technology should solve real problems. We started with phone repairs — the most tangible tech problem people face — and expanded into automation, digital products, and data-driven football intelligence.

Every division of TaurusCore exists to serve one goal: give people and businesses tools that actually work.
